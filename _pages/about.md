I am a Ph.D. student at the [Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences](http://www.ia.ac.cn/), advised by Prof. [Bo Xu](https://people.ucas.edu.cn/~xubo). My research focuses on **multimodal agent post-training**, particularly on enhancing the reasoning and execution capabilities of multimodal large language models for code generation and scientific computing.

I received my B.S. degree from [University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC)](https://www.uestc.edu.cn/) in 2022.
