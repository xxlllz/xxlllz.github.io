We propose AutoReproduce, a multi-agent framework that automatically reproduces AI experiments described in research papers in an end-to-end manner. The framework introduces a Paper Lineage algorithm that extracts implicit domain-specific knowledge from relevant references to address unstated assumptions. AutoReproduce outperforms existing agent baselines on all five evaluation metrics by over 70%.
