We define the novel task of code-based chart editing and construct ChartEdit, a high-quality human-annotated benchmark comprising 1,405 diverse editing instructions applied to 233 real-world charts. The benchmark evaluates 10 mainstream MLLMs, revealing that while large models can generate code to produce images, their ability to generate accurate edits remains limited (SOTA only achieves 59.96 score).
