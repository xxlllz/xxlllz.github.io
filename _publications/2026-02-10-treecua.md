We propose TreeCUA, a tree-structured verifiable evolution framework for efficiently scaling GUI automation. TreeCUA organizes computer-use agent trajectories as reusable exploration trees, combines multi-agent exploration, verification, summarization, and quality evaluation, and introduces TreeCUA-DPO to improve GUI planning from adjacent trajectory branch information.
