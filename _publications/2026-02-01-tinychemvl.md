We design TinyChemVL, an efficient chemical Vision-Language Model with only 4B parameters that outperforms existing models on both molecular and reaction tasks. The model reduces visual tokens to 1/16th of what ChemVLM uses through adaptive visual token pruning and merging, while achieving superior performance. We also introduce ChemRxn-V, the first reaction-level benchmark for vision-based chemical reaction recognition.
